The Origin Story

He lived through it first.

Dr. Newman didn't arrive at stress medicine through a textbook. He personally experienced severe anxiety — and resolved it not through medication, but through targeted changes in sleep, nutrition, and nervous system regulation.

"The brain is not a fixed organ. It can be rewired. My job is to show you how."

Today, his clinical practice focuses on Stress Response Dysfunctions — the underlying neurological patterns behind generalized anxiety, burnout, and chronic illness. His approach combines neuroscience, cognitive behavioral therapy, NLP, and clinical nutrition into a single, coherent system.

He is a Fellow of the American Institute of Stress, a board-certified Holistic Health Practitioner, and has contributed over 30 research articles to leading integrative health publications.
